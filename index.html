Thondaimandalam Foundation was founded in 2018 with the mission to enhance and sustain rural and peri-urban livelihoods, with a focus on natural farming, water conservation, conservation of native biodiversity, enhancing access and quality of education, art and culture in the Thondaimandalam bioregion of Tamil Nadu. Our values are grounded in grassroots collective action, innovation, local self-governance and self-sustaining rural and peri-urban livelihoods.
